Luiz Augusto Wendt

Game developer that wants to make games that will leave people with a happy heart. ♥

Born in 1997 in the countryside of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil, my first contact with programming was when I entered the Game Development course at Unisinos on 2015, learning C++. This kicked off my passion for programming.

The university gave me opportunities to try out different fields. There, I took part at a visualization laboratory and Atomic Rocket Entertainment, an experimental game development studio, which gave me first-hand experience with game development. At Atomic I worked on multiple games and gamification apps developed in Unity.

Leaving university's ground, I worked with software engineering at Owl Bytes, developing an Android SDK in java for an international client. I had hands-on experience with software development, software architecture and scrum. It was a really great and different experience, but I wanted to get back to game development and had the opportunity to work within OKL at Baby Owl Studios. There I lead the programming team and took part of the production for some stages of the development of Star Botanist.

Nowadays I'm a game programmer at Aquiris.
